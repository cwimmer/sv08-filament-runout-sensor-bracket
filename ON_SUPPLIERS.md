# On Components and Suppliers

## Suppliers

In the Bill of Materials, I list various suppliers.  You do not have to purchase the components from the same suppliers.  These parts will fulfill the requirements of this design, but you may select equivalent parts from any supplier.

## Materials

### 3D Printer Filament

I selected ASA as the filament.  You may substitute the material of your choice.  Just ensure you understand the material properties meet your load requirements.
